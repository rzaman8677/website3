About Us


SparcGroupLLC specializes in foreign industries, and financial investments. We have the passion for creating everday reusable, and environmental friendly items. We believe we can meet our customers needs, while not harming the environment. We also have investments in various amount of companies that have great plans. We are taking investments currently, so feel free to email us if you would like to make an investment in us.


At SparcGroupLLC we plan to start production by late 2022 or late 2023. Production will first start in Bangladesh, and we will produce environmentaly friendly spun bond non-woven fabric bags. SparcGroupLLC will export the bags to other countries, starting with the US and later to countries in Europe. Production will be expanded to many more non-woven products by ealry 2024.


SparcGroupLLC also does financial consulting by giving advice to what stocks to buy in order to make money. We have the perfect picks for short term and long term gains. We can give you the best stock picks for the lowest prices out there. We also help consult people interested in crypto-currencies. Our pros have a huge reputation of making good investements that can make you a millionare so email us now if you are interested in financial consulting.
